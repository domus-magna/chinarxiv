üìä Summary & Recommendation

  • Title Color: Use BLACK (#212529), not red. Red on hover only.
  • Layout Density: Keep it COMPACT (like Layout 1) - researchers want to see more papers.
  • Subject Tags: INCLUDE them (like Layout 2), but make them subtle and limit to 2-3 visible.
  • Red Accent: Reserve for INTERACTIVE elements (links, hover states) only.
  • Date Format: Use HUMAN-READABLE format ("7 December 2025"), not ISO timestamp.
  • Metadata: Keep date + authors on ONE LINE for compactness.
